Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

PetHabit is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Pet Information

When you add your pet's profile, we collect information you provide such as your pet's name, breed, birth date, weight, gender, and photos. This information is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Activity Data

We track activities you log for your pet including walks, meals, medications, sleep, and other care activities. This data is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults and local database storage.

Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

If you choose to integrate with Apple HealthKit, PetHabit may read and write health-related data to help you track your pet's health metrics. We access HealthKit only with your explicit permission.

Location Data

When you use walk tracking features, we may collect location data to map walk routes. This data is processed locally and is not stored on our servers.

Usage Information

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app. This data does not include any personal information and is used solely for app analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the PetHabit service
  • Generate health insights and activity reports for your pet
  • Send you reminders for pet care activities
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

All pet-related data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. AI Features

PetHabit uses on-device machine learning (CoreML) for AI-powered health insights and behavior analysis. Your pet data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to cloud-based AI services.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your pet information to outside parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your pet's information stored in the app
  • Delete your pet's data by deleting the app
  • Export your data using the app's export feature
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

7. Children's Privacy

PetHabit is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
